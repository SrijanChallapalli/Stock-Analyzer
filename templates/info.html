Information & Explanations

Technical Indicators

  • MACD: Moving Average Convergence Divergence – indicates trend momentum by comparing two EMAs.
  • RSI: Relative Strength Index – shows if a stock is overbought or oversold (range 0–100).
  • Bollinger Bands: Measure volatility and identify potential price reversals.
  • SMA: Simple Moving Average – smooths out price data to highlight trends.
  • EMA: Exponential Moving Average – similar to SMA but gives more weight to recent prices.

Fundamental Analysis

  • P/E Ratio: Price-to-Earnings – shows how much investors are willing to pay per dollar of earnings.
  • P/B Ratio: Price-to-Book – compares market value to the book value of a company.
  • ROE: Return on Equity – measures profitability relative to shareholder equity.
  • ROA: Return on Assets – indicates how efficiently a company uses its assets.
  • Debt-to-Equity: Indicates a company’s financial leverage and risk.
  • Dividend Yield: The percentage return from dividends.
  • Free Cash Flow: Cash generated from operations, indicating financial health.
  • Revenue Growth: Long-term growth trend of a company’s sales.
  • Profit Margin: The percentage of revenue that turns into profit.

Visualization

The chart is generated using Plotly. It features a candlestick chart for stock prices, with overlays for MACD (and its Signal Line) as well as RSI plotted on a secondary axis (range 0–100) to easily spot overbought or oversold conditions.
